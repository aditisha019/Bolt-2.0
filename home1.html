PHYSICAL HEALTH

Some Healthy Habits

1. Prioritize a balanced diet with a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
2. Stay hydrated by drinking an adequate amount of water throughout the day.
3. Incorporate regular physical activity into your routine, aiming for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week.
4. Ensure sufficient sleep by aiming for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.
